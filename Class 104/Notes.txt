Kind Of Data Which Provides Information Is Known As Desprective Data,
It Is Also Known As Central Tendancy.

Its Examples Are-
1)Mean("Average")
For ex-
A=[23,24,25,26,2,20]
Sum=23+24+25+26+2+20
Mean=Sum/6

2)Mode("Highest Occurance Of A Number")
For ex-
A=[23,24,25,23,25,15,23]
Mode=23

3)Median("Middle Number")
For ex-
A=[23,24,25,24,25,15,23]
Median=24,

A=[23,25,26,30,35,23,46,55]
Median=32.5(30+35/2)
